Introduction

If you've ever wanted to play multi-player Minecraft with your friends, the options that most people consider are:

  • A Minecraft Realms subscription from Microsoft. Minecraft Realms are nice in the sense that it is super easy to get started. However,
    • There is zero world customization
    • A limit is set at 10 playing at a given time.
  • Server hosting, from services such as shockbyte or Apex Hosting.
